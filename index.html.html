About Us

Rooted in Electrical Trade, Built for Global Reach

Kush Enterprises is a Gurugram-based export firm specialising in electrical components and industrial products. Our foundation rests on over a decade of hands-on experience in electrical retail — giving us direct access to verified suppliers, genuine product knowledge, and competitive pricing that many exporters simply cannot match.

We are a fully registered proprietorship with a valid GST number and IEC (Importer Exporter Code) issued by the Government of India — making every transaction transparent, compliant, and trustworthy.

Our mission is simple: connect international buyers with quality Indian electrical products at fair prices, backed by reliable communication and honest dealing.
